PAUL CEZANNE

IMPRESSIONISM | CUBISM

Known for paintings such as The Basket of Apples (1895) and The House of the Suicide (1873), Cezanne is one of the many avant-garde artists that showcases an incredible visual disposition; one that manages to translate people, environments and his surrounding life with purposeful colours and prismatic brushwork. His art uses mediums such as oil paints and watercolours, while focusing on the underlying movements and designs of his subjects in equal parts of hue saturation and vibrancy.
